About This BMW X5

The 2023 BMW X5 xDrive40i is a luxury midsize SUV that combines performance, comfort, and technology. This brand new model features a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

The X5 offers a premium driving experience with its adaptive suspension, precise steering, and powerful braking. Inside, you'll find high-quality materials, comfortable seating for five passengers, and a spacious cargo area. The latest technology features include a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, and a host of driver assistance features.

This particular model comes with the Premium Package, which adds a panoramic sunroof, head-up display, and gesture control. It also includes the Driving Assistance Professional Package, which adds adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, and traffic jam assist.
